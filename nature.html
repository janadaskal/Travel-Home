OHRID

UNESCO Protected City

Ohrid is one of the UNESCO's Cultural World Heritage sites. In the medieval ages, Ohrid had 365 churches, one for each day of the year, and has been referred to as a "Jerusalem (of the Balkans).

KRUSHEVO

A small town perched on top of Europe

Krushevo is the highest town in Macedonia, situated at an altitude of over 1350 m (4429 feet) above sea level. It is known for its 19th-century architecture.
